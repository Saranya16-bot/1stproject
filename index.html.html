About Vaigai College

A team of Industrial experts, with a passion for serving our society through education had established Vaigai Educational Trust (VET) to provide education particularly to the rural students. VET founded Vaigai College of Engineering (VCE) in Therkutheru, Madurai in August 2012.

Vaigai Educational Trust (VET) is committed to ensure continuous development in order to make VCE, a well recognized institute of excellence, on a par with globally renowned technical universities!

VAIGAI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

VCE is a pioneering technical institution, offering B.E undergraduate programs. It is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and Approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college is situated in a serene natural landscape at about 25 kms from Madurai and 5 kms from Melur. The college has a special helicopter facility for aerial sightseeing in and around Madurai.
